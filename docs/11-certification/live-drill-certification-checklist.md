# Guardian Live Drill Certification Checklist

## 1. Purpose

This checklist defines the evidence required to certify Guardian after live operational drills.

Guardian cannot be certified unless required attack scenarios are successfully detected, contained, recovered, and fully documented.

---

## 2. Global Rules

- Every required drill must be executed.
- Every drill must produce evidence.
- Failures must be analyzed before certification.
- Regression updates are mandatory after failures.
