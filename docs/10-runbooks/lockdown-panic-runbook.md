# Lockdown and Panic Runbook

## Purpose

Defines operator steps for lockdown, panic, and emergency containment.

## Steps

1. Confirm emergency condition.
2. Select lockdown scope.
3. Activate lock or panic mode.
4. Verify real Discord permission enforcement.
5. Monitor incident status.
6. Unlock only after safe state is confirmed.
7. Verify restore.

## Success Criteria

Normal users are actually restricted during lock and restored after unlock.
