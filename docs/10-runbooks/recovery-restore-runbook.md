# Recovery Restore Runbook

## Purpose

Defines safe recovery process after destructive incidents.

## Steps

1. Confirm containment.
2. Locate snapshot.
3. Validate snapshot integrity.
4. Generate restore plan.
5. Execute safe restore.
6. Verify restored state.
7. Record partial failures.
8. Produce recovery report.

## Rule

Do not blindly restore dangerous permissions or dangerous roles.
