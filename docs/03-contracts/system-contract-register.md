# System Contract Register

## AntiNuke Contract
Detect destructive guild actions and trigger containment.

## FakePerms Contract
Effective permissions are decided by Guardian authority, not raw Discord permissions alone.

## Bot Authorization Contract
Only explicitly authorized bots may remain in protected guilds.

## Webhook Security Contract
Unauthorized webhooks and integrations must be frozen, deleted, or contained.

## Recovery Contract
Snapshots must support restoration of channels, roles, permissions, and critical structure.

## Logging Contract
Every security decision must produce traceable evidence.
